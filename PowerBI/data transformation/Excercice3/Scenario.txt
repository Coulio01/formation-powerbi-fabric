Import the data from the file folder by clicking Get Data, clicking Excel and clicking Connect.
Select folder you have downloaded and click Open.
Select Sheet1 and click Edit.

In QUERY SETTINGS change Name to Country Population.
On the ribbon, click Remove Rows, click Remove Top Rows, in Number of rows, type 3, and click OK.
On the ribbon, click Use First Row as Headers.

Select the 1999 column, hold Shift and select the 2014 column.
On the Transform ribbon, click Unpivot Columns.
Right-click the Attribute column header and click Rename.

Type Year and press Enter.
Right-click the Value column header and click Rename.
Type Population and press Enter.

Click the drop-down button on the Year header, unselect 1999, and click OK.
Click the Year header, on the Transform ribbon click Data Type, and click Whole Number.
Cick the Population header, on the Transform ribbon click Data Type, and click Whole Number.

Click the Home ribbon.
Click Close & Apply.
